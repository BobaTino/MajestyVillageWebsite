Explore Our Floor Plans

Male Living Space

The male living space is thoughtfully designed with a shared living room for socializing and leisure. It includes up to three rooms with flexible accommodation options (single or shared, up to 2 persons per room). A single restroom is centrally located for accessibility, enhancing the convenience of the layout.

Female Living Space

The female living space features a common area for relaxation and interaction. It includes up to seven rooms, with options for single or shared accommodation (up to 2 persons per room). Two conveniently located restrooms ensure privacy and comfort for all residents. The layout fosters a harmonious balance between personal and shared spaces.
